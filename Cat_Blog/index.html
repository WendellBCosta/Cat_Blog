Could cats be astronauts?

Cats are fascinating animals and surrounded by mysteries. Independent and mysterious in temperament, These felines are known for their agility, elegance and often unpredictable behavior. However, recently, a theory has been gaining ground on the internet and among conspiracy theorists: could cats be astronauts?
The theory suggests that cats are not terrestrial animals, but rather extraterrestrial beings sent to carry out a specific mission on our planet. There are historical reports that point to the fact that cats They were considered sacred beings in different cultures and were even associated with divine figures. This could indicate that cats have a much more mystical origin than previously thought.

Furthermore, there are theorists who believe that felines have telepathic abilities and are capable of to communicate with other alien species. This belief is based on reports from people who claim have seen cats reacting to stimuli that are not perceptible to humans, or even behaving unusually during astronomical events, such as an aurora borealis. These and other signs were interpreted as signs that cats are interplanetary beings.
